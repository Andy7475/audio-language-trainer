Why It Works: The Cognitive Science Behind FirePhrase

  • Lexical Sentence Mining: Learn words in context, not in isolation, making use of the innate ability of the brain to internalise language. For example, "I have made a decision", rather than "to make" and "decision" word and grammar tables
  • Dual-Coding: Pair phrases with specific images to create mental 'hooks' that improve recall by up to 75%.
  • Phonetic Decoding: Practice with normal and slow-speed audio to master pronunciation and natural rhythm.
  • Active Recall: Use fill-in-the-blank cards to strengthen memory retrieval for real-time conversations.

How to Use

Spend 20-30 minutes per day to make steady progress. Each session includes:

  • Listening Cards: Start by hearing the phrases to train your ear for natural rhythm and pronunciation.
  • Reading Cards: Reinforce your understanding by seeing the phrases in written form.
  • Speaking Cards: To build confidence and fluency.

Follow the packs in order to ensure a structured progression through the collection. Explore and enjoy the language with AI-powered learning insights and links to online dictionaries for deeper understanding.
